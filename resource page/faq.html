FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our frequently asked questions to find answers to common queries. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact our support team.

  • How do I update the application?
    To update the application, go to the 'Help' menu and select 'Check for Updates.' Follow the on-screen instructions to download and install the latest version.
  • What are the system requirements?
    The application requires a minimum of 4GB RAM, 2GHz dual-core processor, and 500MB of free disk space. It supports Windows 10 or later, macOS 10.12 or later, and most Linux distributions.
  • How can I report a bug?
    You can report a bug by going to the 'Support' section of our website and filling out the bug report form. Please provide detailed information about the issue you're experiencing.
  • Can I customize the interface?
    Yes, the application allows for extensive customization of the interface. Go to 'Settings' and then 'Interface' to change themes, layout, and other visual options.
  • Where can I find the user manual?
    The user manual is available within the application under the 'Help' menu. You can also download it from our website in PDF format.
  • Is there a mobile version of the application?
    Currently, there is no mobile version of the application. However, we are working on a mobile-friendly version that will be released soon.
  • How do I reset my password?
    To reset your password, go to the login screen and click on 'Forgot Password.' Follow the instructions to receive a password reset link via email.
  • What should I do if the application crashes?
    If the application crashes, try restarting it. If the problem persists, check for updates or reinstall the application. Contact support if you continue to experience issues.
  • How do I backup my data?
    To backup your data, go to 'Settings' and select 'Backup.' Choose the location where you want to save your backup file and follow the prompts.
  • Are there any tutorials available?
    Yes, we have a series of video tutorials available on our website. These tutorials cover everything from basic setup to advanced features.
